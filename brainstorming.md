Brainstorming Ideas for Mobile IOS apps
===

#### Common categories:
* Education
* Lifestyle
* Productivity
* Travel
* Health & Fitness
* Social
* Entertainment

## Ideas

- An app that manages the to do's in your house like getting service for the AC, changing the filters, paying bils, taking the trash, chores.
- Pick a finger/ decission making app, when you don't want to decide or need to pick something at random. Coin flip, Magic ball, pick a finger, shortest stick, team selector, wheel.
- Subscription tracker: One tab to keep track of subscriptions by list, the other tab to keep track of free trials with push reminders.
- Morse code translation app: it uses the flashlight or audio on the phone to send morse code, anther tab for translation by tapping the phone.
- Best quotes: An app that gives you a quote for any situation based on parameters, It allows you to save the quote or write your own, it also allows you to comment on the quote for future reference.
- Indoor climbing app social media, an app where you can post a route and how many tries you did and even the difficulty.

## Evaluation of top two

#### 1.Indoor Climbing Social Media App

Description: A mobile app for indoor climbers to log the routes they climb (difficulty, attempts, notes) and share their progress with friends. It's a digital logbook and social feed for the indoor climbing community.

Category: Social Networking / Fitness & Tracking

Mobile: Uses the Camera to post routes with photos and Location to identify the gym.

Story: Makes logging climbs rewarding by gamifying progress and connecting climbers. It's about celebrating achievements and sharing tips ("beta") within a dedicated community.

Market: Niche but Engaged. Targets the specific, growing group of indoor boulderers and sport climbers. High value for a tight-knit community looking for an easy-to-use simple digital logbook.

Habit: Very High. Usage is tied directly to the physical hobby (climbing sessions, 2-4 times a week). The social feedback (likes, comments) encourages immediate logging after every climb.

Scope:

V1 (MVP): Allow users to create an account and log a route (difficulty, attempts, notes) to a personal, local list. Focus on fast, easy logging UI.

V2: Add photo upload/viewing and a simple feed displaying the user's own recent activity.

V3: Implement social features: follow friends, view a public feed, and add comments (requires a simple backend).

V4: Full Camera integration for in-app photo capture and sharing.

#### 2.Best Quotes App

Description: A tool that finds relevant quotes based on the user's situation/mood. Users can save, organize, and write personal comments on these quotes for future reference.

Category: Productivity / Lifestyle

Mobile: Important for instant access and reminders. Uses Push Notifications for a daily quote reminder. Core function is fast, local storage for personalized quotes and comments.

Story: Serves as a personal wisdom journal. It provides the perfect quote on demand and allows the user to anchor that quote to a specific moment or feeling using the comment feature.

Market: Large and Competitive. Saturated market a lot of quote apps. The unique value is the situational search and the personal commenting feature, which makes it a unique utility.

Habit: Moderate. Driven by the daily Push Notification. Habit increases when the user begins actively using the commenting feature to turn it into a personal tool.

Scope:

V1 (MVP): Find and implement a quotes API. Allow users to search by a few simple tags and display the result. Focus on clear text output.

V2: Add the ability for users to Save a quote and implement the Comment feature.

V3: Introduce the Push Notification system for the daily reminder.

V4: Implement more advanced filtering/search parameters (author, specific situation) and different display themes.
